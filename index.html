Send keystroke to your PC

Macro Keys permits to send keystrokes from your Android smartphone to your PC (Windows, Mac and Linux).

Connect with Bluetooth or Wifi

To send the keystrokes from your smartphone to your PC you can use a Wifi connection or a Bluetooth connection.

Customize keyboards

The included editor can be used to customize or create new keyboards that then can be used on your smartphone.

Customize:

  • The keys appearance
  • The keys to be associated with the pressure of the keys
  • The type of pressure simulations for the keys
  • The keys position

How is composed

Macro Keys is composed of an android app that sends the keystroke to the server, a server that runs on a PC and receive the keystroke received and a PC editor to customize the keyboards that can be used.
